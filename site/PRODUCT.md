# thaw — product context

**What it is:** "git for live agent sessions." thaw freezes a running vLLM/SGLang
session (KV cache + weights + scheduler state) into a durable file you can
checkpoint, branch, diff, and restore. Inspect & diff the snapshot on a laptop,
no GPU. Restore skips prefill.

**Register:** brand / marketing landing page (design IS the product). Audience:
ML infra & RL post-training engineers, agent-framework builders, serverless-GPU
platform teams. Technical, skeptical, allergic to hype.

**Voice (three words):** precise · cold · engineered. The product is a primitive,
not a SaaS. Reads like a systems tool, not a startup.

## Design system (committed — do not relitigate)

- **Type:** Geist Sans + Geist Mono. Display = Geist 600, tight tracking (-0.03em).
- **Palette:** near-black ground (#07080a), single white ink expressed at
  opacities, a chrome metal ramp (echoes the snowflake logo), and ONE accent —
  UV/frost blue (`--uv` #6fb3d9) reserved for CTA, links, focus, live-pulse.
- **Lane:** Vercel pure-black monochrome restraint + chrome. NOT editorial-serif,
  NOT SaaS-cream, NOT acid-maximalism.
- **Surfaces:** hairline rules + one-step tonal lifts. No drop shadows on cards.
  Terminal chrome is the recurring artifact frame.
- **Hero:** locked/approved. Chrome snowflake video backdrop (screen-blended,
  saturated) behind the headline stays as-is.

## Slop to avoid here

Identical icon-card grids, tiny uppercase tracked eyebrow above every section,
01/02/03 numbered section scaffolding, gradient text, side-stripe borders,
hero-metric template. Motion must be intentional, not fade-on-scroll-everything.
